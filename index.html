// Module 00

OSINT — Open Source Intelligence

Before we hack anything, let's learn how attackers gather information — using only publicly available tools. This is called OSINT. No illegal access, no special software. Just Google.

🔍 Google Lens — Reverse Image Search

Did you know you can find where a photo was taken just by uploading it to Google? Try this with your facilitator:

  • Go to images.google.com
  • Click the camera icon
  • Upload any landmark photo
  • Google will identify the location
  • Lesson: photos can reveal your location!
